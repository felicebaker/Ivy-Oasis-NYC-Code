________   About Us   ________

Welcome To Ivy Oasis NYC!

{% for entry in aboutphotos_list %} {% if entry.id == 1 %}

Ivy Oasis NYC launched in 2014 as a platform to bring together Ivy League graduates from Brown, Columbia, Cornell, Dartmouth, Harvard, Princeton, University of Pennsylvania and Yale. Alumni of Berkeley, Caltech, Cambridge, Chicago, Duke, MIT, Northwestern, Oxford and Stanford are also welcome.

{% elif entry.id == 2 %}

Executive Director, Lisa Chau and President, Canberk Dayan lead Ivy Oasis within the 83,000+ member Alumni of the Ivy League network founded by Osman Niazi. Thus, members have unparalleled access to the largest network of Ivy League alumni in the world. Exceptional expertise spans across industries including, but not limited to finance, law, healthcare, entrepreneurship, management consulting, alternative energy, academia and technology.

{% elif entry.id == 3 %}

Ivy Oasis NYC invites qualified candidates to request membership by logging into the site via LinkedIn (see upper-left sidebar menu). Your school affiliation(s) must be indicated on your LinkedIn profile, but you do not need a New York residence.

{% elif entry.id == 4 %}

We look forward to connecting with you socially and professionally! If you would like to contact us directly, please email canberk.dayan@ivyoasisnyc.com, lisa.chau@ivyoasisnyc.com, or IvyOasisNY@gmail.com .

________   Our Mission   ________

{% for entry in missionphotos_list %} {% if entry.id == 1 %}

What we stand for - our brand essence:

Ivy Oasis NYC desires to serve as an oasis to Ivy alumni who seek to connect with others to create unique synergies.

What we believe in - our defining values:

{% elif entry.id == 2 %} We believe in the power of networking and connecting. Only with other people can one pave the road to success.

Who we seek to engage - our target audience(s):

We seek passionate, willing, sincere, helpful and dedicated Ivy alumni who also believe in making new connections, business partners and friends that last a lifetime.

{% elif entry.id == 3 %}

What distinguishes us - our key differentiators:

Enable networking on both professional and social platforms. Facilitate idea generation among alumni and serve as a catalyst for the supply and demand of the alumni.

{% elif entry.id == 4 %}

What we offer - our overarching experience:

Provide different platforms that facilitate Ivy League alumni interaction and networking. We aim to:

{% elif entry.id == 5 %}
  • Broaden our visions via panels and discussions
  • Share our experiences to enlighten and be enlightened
  • Encourage partnerships in industry specific networking
  • Make new friends
  • Give back to the community
{% endif %} {% endfor %}
